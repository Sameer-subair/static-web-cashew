About Us

Majeediya Cashews History

Since 1980, Majeediya Cashews has grown into one of Kerala's most trusted names in cashew processing and exporting. With decades of experience, we have built a solid reputation for delivering premium quality cashew kernels to international markets, especially Dubai and Singapore.

We are proud to have a dedicated team of over 200 skilled employees working towards our shared goal of excellence in cashew production.

Our Business Focus

Our core focus lies in the processing and export of high-quality cashew nuts. We specialize in sourcing, grading, and exporting cashew kernels to global markets, ensuring consistency, flavor, and premium quality in every batch.

Quality Assurance

We maintain strict quality control with dedicated departments in peeling, grading, and shelling. Every batch of cashew nuts is inspected to meet international quality standards, ensuring our customers receive only the best.

Social Responsibility

We have made it our mission to provide job security and continuous employment, especially for workers affected during the company's takeover period. Empowering local communities and creating opportunities remains at the heart of our operations.

Vission

"To promote Kerala cashew kernels globally."

Mission

"The mission of the company is to organize and uplift the cashew industry in the State, support and render assistance to affiliated societies, and create sustainable employment opportunities for women."
